      • Click on the Apple logo in the top-left corner of your screen.
      • Choose "Sleep" from the dropdown menu.

      • While holding the "Control" key, press the "Power" button (or the Eject key if your Mac has one).
      • Release both keys simultaneously. This action will immediately put your Mac to sleep.

    3. Use Hot Corner (if enabled):

      • If you've set up Hot Corners in System Preferences, you can move your cursor to the designated corner to activate sleep mode.

    4. Use Terminal (for advanced users):

      • Open Terminal.
      • Type sudo shutdown -s now and press Enter. You'll need to enter your administrator password to confirm.
